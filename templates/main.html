Game Rules

Rules: How to decide who gets to bat or bowl? A player picks odd or even and says it out loud after which both of them will pick a number and say it out loud and if the sum of the two numbers said by the players is odd or even then the player that picks the number will decide whether he wants to bat or bowl. What is the game? Both players will get their role of batting or bowling. They can pick one number each and reveal it simultaneously. If the number told by both of them is the same then that number will be doubled and added to the score of the batter and if the number picked by the bowler is one more than or less than the number picked by the batter loses a strike and if it is unrelated then the batter gets the number he picked added to his score. After the batter loses all his strikes, the bowler will now be the batter, this batter will have to beat the score set by the batter to win. How many players? If there is more than 1 player on each side, the game will be different, and there will be a captain who will be selected randomly. He will play the game that decides which team will bat first or bowl first after which he will choose what he wants to do and the other players will give him suggestions. He can pick the player that goes to bat or bowl or swap the players(which means he can choose who can bowl or bat at that time among his team) if he wants to in the middle of the match. He can also relinquish his position to the others. There will also be 1v4, 1v5, and 1v6 mismatches in which a single player with 12 15, or 18 strikes (depending on the number of players in the opposing team) faces off against a group of players with three strikes each. If he wins, he will gain more points than a casual game. He can also play against a random number generator against a computer if he wants to.
